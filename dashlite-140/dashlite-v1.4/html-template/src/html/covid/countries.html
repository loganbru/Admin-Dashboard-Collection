About this data
It changes rapidly

This data changes rapidly, so whats shown may be out of date. Table totals may not always represent an accurate sum. Information about reported cases is also available on the World Health Organization & WorldoMeters.info site.

It doesnt include all cases

Confirmed cases arent all cases. They only include people who tested positive. Testing rules and availability vary by country.
